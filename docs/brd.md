# Business Requirements Document (BRD): LabCircle

## 1. Project Background
Diagnostic test reporting currently suffers from fragmentation, lack of standardization, and communication delays. LabCircle addresses this by offering a unified SaaS platform for laboratory information management.

## 2. Business Objectives
- Reduce administrative overhead for medical labs by 30%.
- Decrease the time between sample collection and clinical report delivery.
- Ensure 100% compliance with local and international healthcare regulations.

## 3. Key Stakeholders
- **Lab Owners & Technicians:** Require high-throughput ordering and reporting software.
- **Clinicians & Medical Staff:** Require clean, structured result histories to support diagnosis.
- **Patients:** Require easy download/view options and secure access to historical reports.

## 4. High-Level Scope
- **Diagnostic Order Processing:** Creation, execution, and dispatch of lab orders.
- **Client Billing System:** Handling invoices for B2B clinical partners and direct B2C patient payments.
- **Compliance Registry:** Recording audit histories for all medical record access.

## 5. Success Metrics
- **Active Users:** Number of registered laboratories and active patients.
- **Platform SLA:** 99.9% uptime for core report retrieval endpoints.
- **API Performance:** Report delivery within 2 seconds of technician approval.
